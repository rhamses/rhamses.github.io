Rimaq - Máquinas de Estampar

website

Rimaq is one of the largest print shop vendors with a strong presence in the popular market, doing many marketing activities on open television and popular programs.

The challenge in this case was to update the Site with a new layout, more modern, and with performance. An aggressive wordpress maintenance job was done, removing outdated, old plugins, and including in the code functionalities that some of the plugins used to do before, thus enhancing the optimization and scalability later.

Working with content was also meticulous Because the database already existed and all the content should be updated to the new identity without needing an editing rework. In addition, for mobile devices, an in-depth navigation study was done to direct the flow to the most relevant sections of the customer's audience.
